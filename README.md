# Bellabeat Fitness Analysis

🔗 **View the live Tableau dashboard:** [Open in Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/varshith.kandikatla/viz/BellabeatSmartDeviceUsageDashboard_17827667513220/Dashboard1)

Analysis of Fitbit smart-device usage data to guide Bellabeat's marketing strategy, using Python, SQL, and Tableau.

## Key Insights
- Users are sedentary ~16 hours/day and miss the 10,000-step goal on ~65% of days.
- Activity peaks at noon and in the early evening (5–7pm).
- Users sleep ~7 hours, but ~44% fall short of the recommended amount.
- Device engagement is high (worn ~28 of 31 days), yet only 8 of 33 log weight.

## Recommendations
Movement reminders & step goals, notifications timed to the evening peak, bedtime/sleep coaching, effortless weight logging, and streak/reward features.
